faq

questions, answered.

the four things people ask us most. anything else — email orders@petshop.live and a person replies within a day.

shipping

do you deliver?

around amsterdam we deliver same-week, free on orders over €35. the rest of the netherlands ships with postnl in 2–3 working days for a flat €4.95. prefer no shipping at all? choose pickup and we'll hold your order in store for a week.

returns

what if it's not right?

you have 30 days. unused items with a receipt get a full refund; opened items we swap or turn into store credit. and if your pet flat-out refuses it, tell us — a picky eater counts as a reason.

opening hours

when are you open?

tuesday to saturday, 9:00–18:00, at havenstraat 12, amsterdam. we're closed sunday and monday so the humans — and the shop cat — get a weekend too.

food advice

can you help me choose the right food?

gladly — it's half of what we do all day. bring your pet's age, weight and any sensitivities (or a photo of the current label) and we'll match a food that fits. can't come by? email us the same details and we'll reply with a shortlist.
